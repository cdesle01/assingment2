Puppy Pramanades was founded in 2021 by Gillian Roeca and Cate Desler.

With our combined love for dogs and the extreme need for dog care in the Medford, Massachusetts area, we decided to create Puppy Promenades!

We started out only walking a few dogs, but have quickly grown so much more than that. We are now acitlvey walking and taking care of 12 dogs!

We are so greatful for all the love and support this community has shown us in the short time we have been around and cannot wait to see what the future has to hold for us!
